# Market Dominance Plan - Redact-1

Strategic feature roadmap to achieve market leadership in FOIA/records management for law enforcement.

## Target Market

**Primary:** Small-to-medium police departments (under 500 officers)
**Why:** No competitor combines workflow management + redaction well for this segment

---

## Phase 1: Table Stakes (Week 1)
*Must-have features to be taken seriously*

### 1. Deadline Tracking
- **Priority:** CRITICAL
- **Effort:** 1-2 days
- **Rationale:** Every workflow competitor has this. Without it, we're not a viable option.
- **Scope:** Due date fields, overdue alerts, calendar view, statutory deadline templates

### 2. Batch Export
- **Priority:** HIGH
- **Effort:** 2-4 hours
- **Rationale:** Quick win. All vendors support this. Supervisors need to export completed requests for records.
- **Scope:** Select multiple files/requests, export as ZIP with manifest

### 3. Complete Audit Export
- **Priority:** HIGH
- **Effort:** 2-4 hours
- **Rationale:** Currently partial. Required for compliance and legal defensibility.
- **Scope:** Full activity log export (who, what, when) in PDF/CSV formats

---

## Phase 2: Competitive Parity (Week 2)
*Catch up to established players*

### 4. Complete Supervisor Review Workflow
- **Priority:** HIGH
- **Effort:** 1-2 days
- **Rationale:** Currently partial. Supervisors are the decision-makers who approve purchases.
- **Scope:** Approval queues, rejection with comments, reassignment, review status dashboard

### 5. Analytics Dashboard
- **Priority:** MEDIUM
- **Effort:** 1 day
- **Rationale:** Supervisors need metrics for reporting to leadership and justifying staff.
- **Scope:** Request volume trends, average turnaround time, overdue rate, staff workload distribution

### 6. Cross-Request Search
- **Priority:** MEDIUM
- **Effort:** 1 day
- **Rationale:** Prevents duplicate work. Staff can find similar prior requests.
- **Scope:** Full-text search across all requests and uploaded documents

---

## Phase 3: Differentiation (Weeks 3-4)
*Features that make us the obvious choice*

### 7. Video/Audio Redaction
- **Priority:** HIGH (differentiator)
- **Effort:** 3-5 days (integrating ML services)
- **Rationale:** KEY OPPORTUNITY. Workflow tools don't have it. Redaction tools don't have workflow. We can be the only integrated solution.
- **Scope:** Body cam video blur, audio bleep, face detection, license plate detection

### 8. Offline Redaction Mode
- **Priority:** HIGH (differentiator)
- **Effort:** 1-2 days
- **Rationale:** No competitor offers this. Enables field work without connectivity.
- **Scope:** Download request bundle, work offline, sync when connected

### 9. Public Portal (Requester Self-Service)
- **Priority:** MEDIUM
- **Effort:** 2-3 days
- **Rationale:** Reduces staff time on status inquiries. NextRequest and GovQA have this.
- **Scope:** Submit requests, track status, download completed files, citizen account

---

## Phase 4: Market Leadership (Weeks 5-6)
*Features that make switching unthinkable*

### 10. AI-Assisted Redaction
- **Priority:** MEDIUM
- **Effort:** 2-3 days (API integration)
- **Rationale:** Automate PII detection. Major time saver and accuracy improvement.
- **Scope:** Auto-detect names, SSNs, addresses, faces; suggest redactions for review

### 11. Multi-Agency Collaboration
- **Priority:** LOW
- **Effort:** 2-3 days
- **Rationale:** Enable request routing between agencies (city PD to county sheriff).
- **Scope:** Agency federation, cross-agency search, request handoff

### 12. Statutory Compliance Engine
- **Priority:** LOW
- **Effort:** 1-2 days
- **Rationale:** Auto-apply exemptions based on state FOIA laws (vary by state).
- **Scope:** State law database, suggested exemptions, compliance checklists

## Competitive Positioning

| Strategy | Execution |
|----------|-----------|
| **Price** | Undercut enterprise vendors (target $3,000-8,000/year vs $15,000+) |
| **Simplicity** | No IT department required. Download and run. |
| **Integration** | Workflow + Redaction in one tool (unique in market) |
| **Mobile** | iOS + Desktop already exists. Leverage this. |
| **Speed** | Faster implementation than enterprise tools (days, not months) |

---

## Win Conditions

To achieve market dominance:

1. **Week 1:** Deadline tracking + batch export = minimum viable workflow tool
2. **Week 2:** Full supervisor workflow + analytics = competitive with JustFOIA
3. **Week 4:** Video redaction + offline = only integrated solution for police departments
4. **Week 6:** AI redaction = best-in-class efficiency
